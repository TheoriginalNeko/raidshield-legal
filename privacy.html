Privacy Policy for RaidShield Bot

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord User IDs
  • Message content (for moderation purposes)
  • Server IDs and Channel IDs

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for:

  • Detecting and preventing raids or spam
  • Maintaining moderation logs
  • Improving bot functionality

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for moderation. We do not sell or share your data.

4. Data Deletion

Server administrators can request deletion of stored logs by contacting the Bot owner.

5. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect stored data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Continued use of the Bot after updates means you agree to the new Policy.
